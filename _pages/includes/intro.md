## Biography

I am **Xinpeng Zhao (赵新鹏)**, currently a Ph.D. student in **Management Science and Engineering** at the **College of Systems Engineering, National University of Defense Technology**.

Before that, I received my M.S. degree in **Computer Science and Technology** from **Southwest University** in 2025, and my B.S. degree in **Medical Information Engineering** from **Shandong First Medical University** in 2022.

My research interests include **open-vocabulary 3D scene understanding**, **3D Gaussian Splatting**, **medical image segmentation**, **rough sets**, and **intelligent diagnosis**.

I have published papers as the first author or student first author in venues and journals such as **ICML 2026**, **Neural Networks**, and **Applied Intelligence**.
